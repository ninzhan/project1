League of Legends

League of Legends is a MOBA or Multiplayer Online Battle Arena. Each player picks a champion and then fights on a battlefield which contains monsters, turrets and other summoners. They then fight for control of the field

There are a multiude of champions to play, guaranteeing a game experience for everyone.

Eventually, this will be an analytics page which will allow summoners to upgrade their game. Whether those analytics are in a program or directly on the website is yet to be seen.
